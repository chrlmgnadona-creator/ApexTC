Definition

An Order Block is a specific price range where institutional traders have placed significant buy or sell orders. These zones are often identified by a sharp move away from the area, leaving behind an unmitigated supply or demand imbalance.

How to Identify

  • The Move: Look for a strong impulsive move that breaks market structure.
  • The Candle: Identify the last opposing candle before the impulsive move starts.
  • The Mitigation: Wait for price to return to this range to "test" the remaining institutional orders.
