Trend Trading RIDING MOMENTUM

Trend Identification

Trend trading is a strategy that attempts to capture gains through the analysis of an asset's momentum in a particular direction. The core philosophy is "the trend is your friend."

Core Principles

  • Higher Highs & Higher Lows: The hallmark of an established uptrend.
  • Moving Averages: Used to smooth price data and define the trend bias.
  • Trendlines: Diagonal support or resistance lines that contain price movement within a channel.

Risk Management in Trends

In a trend, the biggest risk is a reversal. Always trail your stop-loss order along the trend line to protect profits while allowing the trend to develop.
