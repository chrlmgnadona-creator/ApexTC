Systematic Execution

Algorithmic models utilize pre-defined rules, mathematical formulas, and statistical analysis to execute trades automatically at high speeds and volumes, removing human emotional bias from the decision-making process.

Key Components

  • Signal Generation: Technical or fundamental inputs that trigger a potential trade.
  • Risk Filters: Automated checks for position sizing, maximum drawdowns, and portfolio exposure.
  • Execution Engine: The logic that routes orders to the market to minimize slippage and transaction costs.

Backtesting & Validation

An algorithm is only as good as its historical validation. Rigorous backtesting against out-of-sample data is essential to identify "over-fitting" and ensure the model remains robust in changing market conditions.
