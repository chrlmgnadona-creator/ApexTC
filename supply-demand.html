Supply & Demand MARKET EQUILIBRIUM

Foundations of Price Action

[Image of economic supply and demand curve model]
Price Quantity

Supply and demand is the fundamental economic model that determines the price of any asset. Price moves toward a point of equilibrium where the quantity supplied equals the quantity demanded.

Core Concepts

  • Supply Zones: Areas on a chart where selling pressure is high, often resulting in a downward reversal or pause in an uptrend.
  • Demand Zones: Areas where buying pressure is high, often resulting in upward price rejection or the initiation of a new trend.
  • Equilibrium: The state where buy and sell orders are balanced, leading to range-bound price action.

Identifying Imbalances

Trading based on supply and demand focuses on identifying zones where price has moved aggressively away from a level. This aggressive movement indicates an imbalance, suggesting that future orders at that same level may encounter similar exhaustion or initiation forces.
