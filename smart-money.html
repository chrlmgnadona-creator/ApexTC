What is Smart Money?

Smart Money Concepts (SMC) focus on identifying the footprint of institutional investors—banks, hedge funds, and market makers—who have the capital to move the market. Instead of relying on traditional retail indicators, SMC analyzes order flow and liquidity.

Key Institutional Concepts

ConceptDefinition
Order BlockAreas where institutional accumulation/distribution occurs.
Fair Value GapAn imbalance in price where one side of the market is uncompensated.
LiquidityPools of stop-loss orders used to fuel institutional entries.
Market Structure ShiftA break in the prevailing trend indicating a reversal of institutional intent.

Institutional Logic

  1. Liquidity Sweep: Institutions often drive price past a support/resistance level to trigger retail stops before reversing.
  2. Mitigation: Price often returns to an Order Block to "mitigate" the leftover institutional orders.
  3. Efficiency: Markets seek to fill "Fair Value Gaps" to return to an efficient price state.
