Tape Reading TIME & SALES

Analyzing Order Flow

Tape reading, also known as Order Flow analysis, is the practice of observing the "Time and Sales" window to identify institutional buying or selling pressure before it manifests on a price chart.

Core Concepts

  • Aggression: Observing whether market orders are lifting the offer (buying) or hitting the bid (selling).
  • Absorption: When large sell orders are met by strong buying interest at a specific level, preventing price from falling.
  • Imbalance: A significant discrepancy in volume between the buy side and the sell side, often indicating a breakout.

The Art of the Tape

Tape reading is a skill developed through experience. It is not about looking for a static pattern, but about "feeling" the rhythm of the market and identifying when a major participant is entering or exiting a position.
