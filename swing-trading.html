Swing Trading MULTI-DAY TRENDS

Capturing Market Swings

Swing trading is a style of trading that attempts to capture gains in a stock or any financial instrument over a period of a few days to several weeks. Swing traders use technical analysis to look for trading opportunities within a trend.

Core Principles

  • Timeframe: Positions are typically held for several days to weeks, reducing the stress of intraday decision-making.
  • Risk Management: Because positions are held overnight, swing traders must be aware of "gap risk" and news events.
  • Trend Following: Focuses on identifying the direction of the medium-term trend and entering during temporary retracements.

Fundamental Rules

Success in swing trading requires patience to wait for the correct setup, a clear understanding of the broader market context, and the discipline to let winning trades develop over time.
