Day Trading INTRADAY EXECUTION

Intraday Market Dynamics

Day trading involves the buying and selling of financial instruments within the same trading day. All positions are closed before the market close to avoid overnight risk.

Key Focus Areas

  • Volatility: Day traders thrive on price fluctuations to create profit opportunities.
  • Liquidity: Choosing highly liquid assets ensures quick entry and exit without significant slippage.
  • Market Hours: Focus is placed on high-volume sessions, typically the market open and close.

Fundamental Rules

Successful day trading relies on a strict trading plan, consistent risk-to-reward ratios, and the avoidance of emotional decision-making during high-volatility periods.
