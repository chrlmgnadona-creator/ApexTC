Risk Management CAPITAL PRESERVATION

Position Sizing

Never risk more than 1-2% of your total account equity on a single trade. This ensures longevity and protects against consecutive losses.

Recommended Risk Per Trade: 2%

Risk Parameters

Concept Importance
Stop-Loss Mandatory exit point to invalidate trade setup.
Risk/Reward Minimum 1:2 ratio for sustainable growth.
Drawdown Limit total account decline to under 10%.
Psychology Emotional detachment during active trade management.
