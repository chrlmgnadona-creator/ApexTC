Understanding the Levels

Support and Resistance are the most fundamental concepts in trading. They represent the price levels where a trend is likely to pause or reverse due to the concentration of buying or selling interest.

  • Resistance: Price ceiling where selling pressure exceeds buying interest, causing prices to decline.
  • Support: Price floor where buying interest is strong enough to overcome selling pressure, preventing further declines.
  • Role Reversal: Once a resistance level is broken, it often becomes a new support level.
