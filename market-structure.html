Market Structure MARKET ANALYSIS

Trend Identification

Market structure is defined by the sequence of "Higher Highs" (HH) and "Higher Lows" (HL) in an uptrend.

Market Phases

Phase Characteristics
Accumulation Price moves sideways; institutions build positions.
Markup Strong trend movement; buyer dominance.
Distribution Sellers begin entering; price volatility increases.
Markdown Aggressive selling; rapid price decline.
