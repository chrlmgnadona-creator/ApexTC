Breakouts MOMENTUM SHIFTS

Capitalizing on Volatility

A breakout occurs when an asset's price moves outside a defined support or resistance level with increased volume. It signals a shift in market sentiment and the potential start of a new trend.

Key Indicators

  • Volume Confirmation: A true breakout is almost always accompanied by a surge in trading volume.
  • Support/Resistance Levels: The more times a level has been tested, the more significant the breakout when it finally fails.
  • Volatility Expansion: A sudden increase in price range often signals that the market is beginning a trending phase.

The "Fakeout" Warning

Not all breakouts hold. Many price moves cross a level only to snap back quickly. Traders often look for a "retest" of the broken level, confirming it has flipped from resistance to support (or vice-versa), to filter out false signals.
